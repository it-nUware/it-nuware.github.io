CartoonifyLite privacy policy

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application CartoonifyLite (“Application”) for mobile devices that was created by nUware. The Application let the user take cartoonified picture with ease. It does not collect data of any kind and never will.

 

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The Application does not collet data of any kind (personal, non-personal, location, etc...).

 

Permissions

The Application will ask your permission to certain resources and functionalities of your device. These permissions are necessary for the application to function as intended:

  • permission to access your camera: 
    		• this is necessary to take a picture using the camera of your device.
  • permission to write files on your device: 
    		• this is necessary in order to save the cartoonified picture on your device.

    The pictures never leave your device. We do not share your pictures with anyone. No picture is transferred to our servers or anywhere else.
